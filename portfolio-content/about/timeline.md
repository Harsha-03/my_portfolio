---

## How He Works

Harsha does not start by asking how to make something look better.

He starts by asking:

- Where is the user getting confused?
- What moment creates friction or mistrust?
- What information is missing?
- What state is invisible?
- What decision does the interface need to support?
- What should the user understand without extra explanation?

His design loop is:

1. Identify the real break in the experience
2. Understand user behavior and product context
3. Map the flow
4. Design the interaction
5. Prototype and test
6. Ship or document the solution
7. Iterate based on what the product reveals

Design does not ship perfect. It ships, gets used, reveals what is wrong, and improves.
