## Phase 4 — Active UX Practice (Current)

**Jan 2026 – Mar 2026 | LifeOS | Self-Initiated Product Design Case Study**

- Designed a behavioral productivity system around one thesis: adaptation, not motivation
- Target users: students and early-career professionals aged 20–28
- 4-layer architecture: Decision → Action → Feedback → Learning
- 7 screens designed
- Core decisions: hard cap of 1–3 priorities, Reality Check field, honest empty state

**Apr 2026 | Starbucks Mobile Order Redesign | Self-Initiated UX Case Study**

- 2-week redesign — replaced single "Preparing" state with a 4-state model
- States: In Queue → Being Made → Ready → Waiting
- Insight: users don't want a faster app — they want truth about when the barista starts their drink
- Published on Behance: behance.net/gallery/250629345

**Feb 2026 – Present | Community Dreams Foundation | UI/UX Designer (Volunteer)**

- Designing end-to-end UX for the DreamStream platform
- Wireframes, prototypes, high-fidelity UI in Figma
- Usability testing and iteration based on real feedback
- Agile workflow, Slack-based remote team
