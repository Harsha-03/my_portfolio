## Design Approach

Harsha applies a user-centered workflow at Community Dreams Foundation:

1. Understand community and business goals through stakeholder conversations
2. Map user flows and identify friction points
3. Create wireframes and iterate with PMs before moving to high-fidelity
4. Build interactive prototypes in Figma for developer handoff
5. Conduct usability testing (tested with 10–20 users for vulnerability discovery)
6. Iterate based on real feedback, not assumptions
