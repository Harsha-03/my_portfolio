# Areksoft Technologies — Early Engineering Experience

## Overview

Areksoft Technologies was my first professional exposure to how a technology company operates beyond coursework. The experience provided hands-on visibility into client interaction, technical delivery, collaboration, and how engineering connects to business outcomes.

This experience was formative and directly influenced my decision to pursue independent client work soon after.

---

## Timeline and Context

- **Period:** August 2022 – December 2022
- **Location:** India
- **Academic stage:** Fourth-year Bachelor’s student

---

## Technical Exposure

### Frontend

- HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- building and modifying basic user interfaces
- learning layout and responsiveness fundamentals

### Backend (First Exposure)

- Python and Flask
- understanding request–response flows
- learning how backend services connect to frontend applications

---

## Business and Client Exposure

- observed how clients are identified and approached
- learned how networking influences opportunities
- understood how technical services are explained to non-technical clients

---

## UI/UX and Outreach Learning

- worked on posters and outreach materials
- gained basic UI/UX exposure for communication and engagement
- used Figma and Canva
- observed how social media reach and targeting affect visibility

---

## Impact on Career Direction

This experience reshaped my perspective by showing:

- how companies function day-to-day
- how engineering work connects to outcomes
- why ownership matters beyond assigned tasks

It directly motivated me to co-found BuiltinTech.
